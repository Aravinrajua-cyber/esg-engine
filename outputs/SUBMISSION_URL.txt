TO DEPLOY AND CAPTURE THE URL (run locally, in a terminal with your Vercel login):

    cd C:\Hackathon\esg-engine\site

    vercel login           # one-time, interactive
    vercel --prod          # deploys; prints the production URL

Then replace the line below with the printed URL:

    LIVE_URL: <paste https://...vercel.app here after deploying>
